Criteria
  • Schedule cannot be added or updated on month December.
  • Differance between Start Date and Closure Date must be at least 15 days.
  • Differance between Closure Date and Final Closure Date must be at least 3 days.
  • Cannot add Schedule for Next or Previous year.
How it Works?
  • Start Date: Students will be able to upload their contributions for magazine after the defined Start Date
  • Closure Date: Students will not be able to upload new contributions after the defined Closure Date but they can modify the contributions.
  • Final Closure Date: Students will not be able to modify the contributions after the defined Final Closure Date.
  • Publication Status: Students will not be able to upload or modify contribution if the Publication Status is Unpublished. Setting Publication Status to Published will enable contribution collection system working.
